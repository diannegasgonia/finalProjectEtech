SONGS

DESCRIPTIONS

1. "Attention" - 3:00

2. "Hype Boy" - 2:59

3. "Cookie" - 3:55

4. "Hurt" - 2:57

New Jeans is the self-titled debut EP by NewJeans. It was released on August 1, 2022 with "Attention", "Hype Boy" and "Cookie" as the album's triple lead singles.

1. "Ditto" - 3:05

"Ditto" is the first digital single by NewJeans. It was released on December 19, 2022, as a pre-release single for the group's first single album "OMG".

1. "OMG" - 3:32

2. "Ditto" - 3:05

"OMG" is the first single album by NewJeans. It was released on January 2, 2023 with "OMG" serving as the single's title track. The song "Ditto" was pre-released on December 19, 2022.

1. "Zero" - 2:34

"Zero" is the first promotional single by NewJeans. It was released on April 3, 2023, as a promotional single for Coca-Cola's Korean campaign.
